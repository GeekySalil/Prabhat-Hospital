Who We Are

A Legacy of Care & Healing

Established in 2017, Prabhat Hospital is a trusted multi-speciality and trauma care center dedicated to delivering quality healthcare through experienced doctors, advanced diagnostics, and compassionate treatment. With modern facilities and round-the-clock emergency support, we strive to provide safe, accessible, and patient-focused healthcare services.

Our Mission

To provide affordable, accessible, and comprehensive healthcare services to all sections of society with utmost dedication and ethics.

Our Vision

To be the most trusted healthcare provider in Varanasi, renowned for clinical excellence, advanced technology, and compassionate patient care.

Core Values

Integrity, Transparency, Patient-Centricity, Excellence in Care, and Continuous Innovation in Medical Practices.
